Dear Theo,

The photograph after J. Breton and the one after Corot are for Helvoirt; the rest is for you.

Please send the ones for Helvoirt on when you have a chance; however, I will do my best to get another copy of that Rembrandt, so perhaps you had better postpone the forwarding until I have sent you that one, too.

Surely you will have received the letter I wrote yesterday.

With kind regards,

Vt.
