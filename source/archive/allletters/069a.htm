[Also enclosed in Letter 69]

Dear Theo,

You can well imagine how delightful it was meeting Vincent again. I am so glad he is meeting all of them here, too, for no one can imagine what a happy life I lead here, surrounded by so much love. It will be extremely hard to say goodbye to them all. Perhaps I shall stay here until Christmas.

Thanks for your last letter; the situation you write about is not what I am looking for. I shall probably stay in England for a while now. And now goodbye; I do hope to see you this summer, how lovely that vacation time is drawing so near.

Vincent is busy showing engravings to the children.

With much love,

Yours, Anna
